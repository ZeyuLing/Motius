Evaluator Card

Official quality

First-root-anchored SMPL-24 joints. FID_k and FID_g use 1,320 public AIST++ reference motions and GT-derived normalization.

Motius FID

Canonical SMPL-22 joints66 at 30 fps, capped at 20 seconds, encoded by the universal joint-position TMR and L2-normalized before FID.

Beat alignment

Motion beats are speed minima after 83.3 ms Gaussian smoothing. Music-to-motion distance uses a 50 ms kernel in seconds.

Physical motion

Jitter, floating, and sliding are measured on the shared 22-joint body and reported as diagnostics.

Paper Parity

Paper-reported values are audit targets and never participate in ranking.
