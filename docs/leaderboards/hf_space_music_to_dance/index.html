Evaluator Card

Official quality

First-root-anchored SMPL-24 joints. FID_k and FID_g use 1,320 public AIST++ reference motions and GT-derived normalization.

Motius FID

Canonical SMPL-22 joints66 at 30 fps, capped at 20 seconds, encoded by the universal joint-position TMR. Embeddings are L2-normalized before FID.

Beat alignment

Motion beats are speed minima after 83.3 ms Gaussian smoothing. Music-to-motion distance uses a 50 ms Gaussian kernel in seconds.

Physical motion

Jitter, floating, and sliding are measured on the shared 22-joint body. They remain diagnostics until enough methods establish stable ranking ranges.

Paper Parity

Reported paper values are retained as audit targets and are excluded from ranking.
